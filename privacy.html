Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

At History Reimagined, we take the privacy of our visitors seriously. This policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how we use it to provide a better experience for our historical diet enthusiasts.

1. Data Collection & Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track how visitors interact with our site. This helps us understand which historical eras or figures are most popular. This data includes IP addresses, browser types, and time stamps, but does not identify you personally.

2. Mediavine Advertising

History Reimagined partners with Mediavine to serve advertisements. Mediavine uses cookies to serve ads based on your interests and previous visits. You can learn more about how they manage data here: Mediavine Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to store your preferences and optimize your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

If you are visiting from the European Union or California, you have the right to access, delete, or correct any data we hold. Since we do not require accounts or logins, we typically hold no personal identifying information beyond anonymous traffic data.
